School Facility

Dale senior high school provides various school facilities to support and support activities in the school environment. Dale senior high school facilities are very diverse ranging from libraries, parks, clinics, auditoriums, basketball courts, soccer fields, laboratories, and many more. Dale SHS will always strive to develop and improve the quality of school facilities so that they can always provide excellent service.

Library

This library was founded in 2020 when this building was just finished and then is still operating until now. This library also provides a book-borrowing system by showing a student ID card that is addressed to the librarian. This library consists of 3 floors containing books from various old and newest sources.

Clinics

Dale Senior High School has a clinic located near the library. This clinic has been established since 2020 when this building was finished. at the clinic we provide several expert doctors who are ready to treat sick students, or other employees. This clinic is open from 7 am to 3 pm only.

Auditoriums

This auditorium has been around since this building was built in 2020. The auditorium is located with the basketball court building behind it. We often use this auditorium for big events such as seminars, art performances, graduations, and others. In this auditorium building also available facilities such as changing rooms, bathrooms, toilets, halls containing tables and chairs to rest.

Basketball Courts

Dale senior high school also has an indoor basketball court for extracurricular basketball practice or others who need a place for indoor practice. we also like to hold inter-school basketball tournaments that play on this field, so it's safe not to be exposed to rain and heat from outside. The facilities on this basketball court are changing rooms, bathrooms, toilets, and drink machines.

Soccer Fields

We also have our own football field which coincides with the library building to the right. This soccer field can be used by anyone who wants to use it, this field is usually used by ball extracurriculars who usually practice from 3 pm to 5 pm. we also like to hold inter-school ball tournaments which are held on this field as well.

Laboratories

Dale senior high school also has a laboratory for student practice and is also commonly used by science extracurriculars. in this laboratory we provide a variety of very complete science tools for the needs of students and others, in order to facilitate student practicums which are usually held every month.

Parks

we have a very large garden in the front yard of the school to pamper students' eyes or make students relax and be able to relax in the garden. the park is also a lot of chairs and tables to relax or rest for students or others.
