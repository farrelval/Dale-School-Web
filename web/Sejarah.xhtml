History

Dale Senior High school was established in 2020 on an area of ​​20,000 m2 with a Decree of Establishment Number: 112.A/III.01/HK/2012 April 20, 2020. At the beginning of its establishment (2020), the new Senior High School had 5 units of buildings. which consists of Office Building, Workshop, Student WC, Study Room, and Library. In 2021, several building units will be added, consisting of the Computer Laboratory Building and New Classrooms. The total area of ​​the buildings in Dale Senior High School is ± 8,000 square meters.

The school land and buildings are wholly owned by the state. Initially, the school area belonged to the Citayam Government, then due to the desire of the Ciatayam community for a Senior High School to be established in their village, the 2 hectare land was granted. At the beginning of its establishment, Dale Senior High School was led by Mr. Daffa Putra Setyatama S.kom, M.Kom., as the principal. The Principal is assisted by the Deputy Principal (Waka) of the School which includes the fields of Curriculum, Student Affairs, Public Relations, and Infrastructure (Sarpras). Since 2020, those who are in charge of the Curriculum Waka are Mr. Farrell Valentino Awewra, Ms. Hermawati, S.Pd., as Waka Hubin, Ms. Sri Hartati, S.Pd., as Deputy Head of Student Affairs, and Ms. Dini Arista, S.Pd., as Deputy Head of Sarpras.

Astronomically Dale Senior High School is located at 5°21”16″ South Latitude 105°04”14.6″ East Longitude and geographically Dale Senior High School is located in Citayam Depok,. It is ±5 km from the center of the Negerikaton sub-district, ±10 km from the city of Depok, and ±25 km from Jakarta (the capital of the province of Jakarta). Dale Senior High School has a calm, beautiful, cool environment, has beautiful natural scenery, far from noise, so it is very conducive to the implementation of the learning process.

The majors at Dale senior high school are also very diverse, ranging from Social Sciences, Natural Sciences, Indonesian Language, Religion, Computers, Design, Machinery, and Tourism. But the favorite major at Dale senior high school is the science department, because every year there are so many enthusiasts and also produce the best graduates from the science department. Therefore, we open a specialization program starting from grade 2, so that students can choose majors with the passion they have. Dale senior high school also provides several extracurriculars, namely, science, literature, saman dance, basketball, soccer, futsal, badminton, volleyball, Islamic spiritual guidance, Christian spiritual guidance, choir, and paskibraka.
