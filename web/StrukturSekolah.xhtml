those are some names of the school structure and his position at Dale Senior High School

Principal

Farrel Valentino Awewra

The principal is an important part of the structure within a school, starting from the basic education level to the secondary education level. The principal is a teacher who is given the task and mandate as a leader who carries out all forms of school activities, both operational activities, or non-operational activities related to the school and its structure.

Assisstant Principal

Daffa Putra Setyatama

The assistant principal is to assist the principal's business and play a role in improving the quality of service by providing consideration, direction, and support for personnel, facilities, and infrastructure as well as supervision at the level of the education unit (school). Assisstant Principal of Students Affairs

Preti Septika

The Assistant Principal of Students Affairs is responsible to the principal in the field of student affairs and is in charge of developing student development programs and activities/OSIS.

Assisstant Principal of School Finance and Operations

Sri Hartati

Assistant Principal of School Finance and Operations is an official appointed by the Principal and is responsible to the Principal in leading, planning, developing, directing, coordinating, supervising, and controlling school activities in implementing infrastructure programs in accordance with the vision, mission, and established work program.

Assisstant Principal of Academic Affairs and Curriculum

Agus Wahyudi

The following are the duties and functions of the Assistant Principal of Academic Affairs and Curriculum: Develop teaching programs (Annual and Semester Programs) .Creating an Education Calendar .Prepare a decree for the division of teacher teaching tasks and other additional tasks .Making a lesson schedule .Developing the Program and Schedule for the Implementation of the Final School / National Examination .Developing criteria and requirements for students to advance to class/not and pass/fail students who take the exam .Arranging a schedule for receipt of educational report books (Raport) and receipt of STTB/Ijasah and STK .Provide syllabus for all subjects and examples of lesson plans format Provide class agendas, picket agendas, entry/exit permits, teacher agendas (which contain: lesson schedules, study contracts with students, student attendance, meeting notes forms and teacher materials, grade lists, and home visit forms) Preparation of teaching and learning programs and subject analysis.

Assisstant Principal of Public Relations and Partnerships

Andi Saputra

The Assistant Principal of Public Relations and Partnerships is responsible to the principal in the field of industrial, university, and community/stakeholder cooperation. The responsibilities of the Deputy Principal for Public Relations include: , Arrange work programs and PR budgets , Assist committees in school development , Facilitate relations between school members and committees , Coordinate the implementation of school promotions , Map DU / DI , Coordinate the implementation of work practices industry (prakerin) ,Coordinate the implementation of productive competency exams ,Coordinate the tracing of graduates.

Head of Administration

Rendi Oktora

The following are the duties and functions of the Head of Administration: Formulating the goals to be achieved from school administration/administration Compiling administrative work programs/school administration to achieve administrative goals Implementing personnel administration, finance, facilities and infrastructure, student affairs, curriculum , and public relations , Implementing correspondence and filing , Implementing an ICT-based school administration/management information system , Implementing general administration/ correspondence in and out of school , Coordinating the preparation of employee salary lists , Managing activity records and , Compiling monthly, semi-annual, and annual reports.
