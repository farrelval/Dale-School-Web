Dale Senior High School

A favorite high school located in Citayam, has produced some excellent graduates who go on to top universities. There are also many facilities available at Dale High School, namely libraries, parks, clinics, auditoriums, basketball/ball courts, laboratories, and many more. The available tutors are great and experienced teachers and there are plenty of them available at Dale senior high school. every year we also often hold other inter-school events or competitions aimed at strengthening the ties of brotherhood between schools and their students. we also hold art performances that are no less exciting by bringing in world-renowned artists.
